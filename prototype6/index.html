  • High Concept: You, an aspiring mafia boss thrown into a fantasy world, have to defeat the dragon to win the favor of the village!
  • Theme: Fantasy, 1900s Mafia
  • Mandated Variety: This is a shoot-em-up, bullet-hell style game that incorporates random bullet generation
  • Prototype Goal: Bullet physics and sprite collision. The dragon is also animated.
  • Player Experience Goals: Tension and a sense of satisfaction when dodging bullets.
  • Gameplay: Players can use WASD keys to move the character. Fire a bullet using SPACEBAR. The gun has a reload time of 5 seconds, so shoot wisely! Press E to spawn a bodyguard to take the hit for you! You can have a maximum of 3 bodyguards active at one time.
  • Strategies: What player strategies do you expect will be effective at playing this game?
  • Story/Setting/Premise: You are a mafia gang who stumbled upon a way to a fantasy realm. In order to take advantage of the kingdom and it's resources, you are trying
  • to gain the favor of the people by taking on the role of an adventurer.
  • Target Audience: Anyone who enjoys a shooting game.
  • Play Time: 3 minutes.
